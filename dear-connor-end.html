Dear Connor

I wake up
the early morning light creeps in
your peaceful existence draws me closer
as I lean over and touch your skin
waves roll over me
I reach further
holding you tight
everything else falls away

I love you so much my Con
I love each moment we spend together
in the waves
amongst the trees
under the Stars
squished into our little van bed

I love how you are fiercely loyal to those you love
I love how you give your love freely and openly
the energy that surrounds you fills the air with magnetism
bringing everyone closer

I love how you dive head first towards your goals
I love how you pursue them unabashedly
without fear
drawn by passion

I love you for being the strongest wahine around
I love you for being able to be vulnerable
this makes you so powerful
but you already know that
I love that you know that

I love how it feels to hold you close
I love how it feels to hold your body against mine
moments of pure connection
all of the physical world has disappeared
we are lost
together
bound by love
existing
only to share
love and pleasure


In every moment we share together whether it be joyful, exciting, boring, painful or tumultuous. There is love, and it makes it all ok. The last 4 years have been eventful and painful, but you have made them the best period of my life, your presence, your energy, your love.
